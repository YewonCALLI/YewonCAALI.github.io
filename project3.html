1000 of Plastic

In the middle of the North Pacific is a plastic waste island. Plastic Island is more than 90% plastic. These microplastics cause ecosystem disturbances. Microplastics are eaten as food by living organisms, which pose a great danger to sea creatures.

- Colorful Plastic Trash
It expresses various kinds of sea garbage. Using as much color as possible, it expresses that by-products of human household goods flowed out of sea waste.

- Flowing Rubbish
It expresses the flow of trash with seawater. When you click the mouse, trash is made and it expresses the movement that flows with seawater.
