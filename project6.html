Visualize Sound of Stadium

Sound
Yuna Kim Les Miserables
(No commentary, only stadium sounds)

I wanted to express the atmosphere and music of the game scene in three dimensions. I thout it was meanigful because it was a sound that only that moument could contain.

Target data
The sound of the stadium is classified by frequency so that it can have a three-dimensional effect. In addition, the figure varies depending on the intensity of each frequency so that the vivid atmosphere of the field can be transmitted.

Interaction
Control the volume of sound You can see the different figure by the volume of sound.
See the distance between sounds You can see the distance number in screen. Distance is the difference between the volume of two frequency. Also, it is difference of height between two frequency blocks.
